Lead Architect &
Java Developer

Department: IT Development and application support.

As a Lead Architect candidate will be responsible for a high level design choices for the API, software structure, frameworks, database architecture and development of a software solutions. Maintenance of and enhancements to existing software solutions.

As a Lead Java Developer candidate will be responsible for the development of new software solutions as well as the maintenance of and enhancements to existing software solutions.

The candidate will effectively communicate with product team to uncover immediate/future needs, will convey those results to our developers in a meaningful way, and will deliver solutions in a proactive, fast-paced, agile environment.

  • Expected skills
  • Optional skills
  • Key responsibilities
  • Strong Agile mindset and methodology experience, being proactive to help the team to overcome challenges that are encountered.
  • Proven experience in designing and developing of a software with high performance requirements: High availability and performance tuning [3+ years of exp.].
  • Good knowledge in Java Version 7.0 (or later) and applicable design patterns [4+ years of exp.].
  • Experience in J2EE, Spring (MVC, Core), ORM (Hibernate/Eclipselink) [6+ years of exp.].
  • Proven working experience with: Tomcat, Wildfly, Weblogic, TomEE application servers [4+ years of exp.].
  • Good understanding of OOP/OOD design patterns and principles.
  • Proven working expertise with Web Services: RESTful, SOAP, etc. [4+ years of exp.].
  • Strong experience in continuous delivery/integration and application life-cycle management. Working experience with the tools related: Git, Maven, Gradle, Jenkins, Sonarqube, etc.
  • Strong experience with relational databases: Oracle, PostgreSQL [4+ years of exp.].
  • Working experience with TDD/BDD: Junit, JDehave, Cucumber, Spok, Arquillian, etc.
  • *Working experience with security frameworks: Apache Siro, Spring Security, etc.
  • *Working experience with AWS, in particular with Oracle DB set up [1+ years of exp.].
  • *Working experience with AKKA/SCALA [1+ years of exp.].
  • *Strong proven working experience and interest in mentoring of the entire team (in particular remotely and/or multicultural).
  • *Working experience in IT/Tech. software design for CardNotPresent(CNP) Payment Processing/FinTech. area of business [3+ years of exp.].
  • *Working experience in development of the financial services software compliant to the related standards (PCI DSS, PA DSS).
  • *English level: Intermediate+/(B2+) or higher.
  • Exceptional communication skills and the ability to clearly articulate information (both verbal and written).
  • *A proven track record of driving and defining complex IT design, of successful managing of legacy enterprise system migrations, defining and managing a creation and implementation of new software product(s) and/or overseeing enterprise system integration projects.
  • Being fast-learner, eager to learn and develop new things, based on the business reasonability of the project.
  • SCRUM working experience.
  • Working experience with the next technologies: Javascript, AngularJS, React, Meteor, etc. Solid understanding of dynamic, web-based development concepts, including HTML, JavaScript, Java Server Pages, Java Server Faces.
  • Working experience in operation under ADC/Forensic programms.
  • Working experience with performance management tools: JProfiler, Java Flight Recorder, JConsole, etc.
  • Experince with Attlasian software suite: Jira/Confluence.
  • Working experience with BPM engines: jBPM, Activiti, Bonita, Camunda, etc.
  • Working experience with NoSQL databases: MongoDB, OrientDB, Neo4J, etc.
  • Experience with Unix/POSIX systems is a plus.
  • Ability to manage multiple questions simultaneously.
  • To lead software design meetings and analyze user needs to determine technical requirements.
  • Work closely with Product&Business team representatives to support cient, product, and service goals.
  • Be the primary liaision of the software development team, specificaly during planning and analysis phases, as well as for Tech. design related matters, in particular support in Security and PCI DSS compliance audit.
  • Formulate operational plans to achieve business goals by fostering innovation, prioritizing work, aligning resources and leading for results on time.
  • Establish and document best practices, standards, key performance indicators, internal controls and processes for development initiatives.
  • Actively drives the development life-cycle from requirements analysis, feasibility estimation, design, code, documentation, testing, implementation and support.
  • Ensure design specifications are fully and correctly defined and documented.
  • To lead software development efforts, initiatives and technology decisions.
  • Assess technical needs, design and lead development of potential solutions.
  • Research and evaluate new technologies.
  • Meets objectives by leading development projects through entire software development lifecycle.
  • Ensure software design objectives are achieved by communicating with project stakeholders, to advise project status, clarify specifications and answer technical questions.
  • Lead and support enhancements and/or operational changes by analyzing both software and system impact.
  • Define and enforce the company procedures and standards in the testing, implementation, documentation and placement of software into production.
  • Ensure enhancements and changes to the system are technically sound and adhering to overall architecture by leading design and coding reviews.
  • Projecting and development of core engine, systems and services.
  • Projecting, development and integration with gateways of partners or service providers.
  • Projecting and development of security services and associated risk analysis.
  • Communication with Business Domain, validating project needs and plans, other business initiatives.
  • Design and develop high-volume, low-latency applications for mission-critical systems, delivering high-availability and performance.
  • Contribute in all phases of the development lifecycle.
  • Write well designed, efficient code. Contribute and lead development team compliance with the programming/coding policy.
  • Ensure designs are in compliance with specifications.
  • Prepare and produce releases of software components.
  • Support continuous improvement by investigating alternatives and technologies and presenting these for architectural review.
  • Divide tasks into quantifiable subtasks and reasonably predict the effort to complete those subtasks
  • Make sure in proper development of a user documentation and user training.
