Company

CommerceGate offers award winning Merchant Accounts, Payment Gateways, Risk Management and End User Support to discerning webmasters all over Europe. We let you accept payments in all key currencies from every important market.

CommerceGate was founded in 2006 by industry veterans looking to offer better payment processing to online merchants. With a bespoke processing platform and state of the art risk management we quickly grew to become one of the most successful and reliable payment service providers in Europe, trusted by merchants, banks and end users alike.

Still going strong today, we take the complexity out of payment processing and make transactions transparent and secure, allowing you to focus on growing your business and maximising your revenue. With our focus on Service, Security, Speed and Simplicity, we are your ideal payment processing partner with a brand that inspires trust in your end users.

To find out why we are the ideal billing processor for you and to learn more about the benefits of our services, contact us today. We look forward to talking to you!
